E-Flashcard WebApp

Problem/Data Domain

In academic settings, a prevalent challenge is students' difficulty in maintaining focus and developing effective learning methods. To address this, our proposal centers on creating an integrated e-flashcards web application that incorporates a study timer, study music, and customizable features, providing an efficient solution to enhance student’s learning and productivity.

Although other study apps do exist, a large portion of the apps only have one functionality. Due to this, students have to install and use multiple apps and services to gain access to productivity tools. Opening and closing multiple apps can be a major hassle and it also provides an easy opportunity for students to get sidetracked from their studying.

Currently, on the market, there are flashcard apps and study tools such as Quizlet and Anki. However, Quizlet capitalizes off of students by cluttering its platform with ads and hiding essential study tools behind a monthly subscription for Quizlet Plus. The most simplified version of a flashcard app, Anki, however, is a large desktop application with a less-than-appealing user interface and learning curve. Additionally, both applications don't have the modern interests that students look for when studying, such as integrated music and timers.

Additionally, with the internet’s ease of accessibility, we often find ourselves watching internet videos or scrolling through social media when we are supposed to be studying, working on homework, or simply doing anything important. With our flashcard app, we can tackle this issue by providing an all-in-one space where users can study effectively and productively while limiting potential distractions.

App Description

The primary users of the application are students seeking effective study tools to enhance their learning experience, improve productivity, and establish a structured study routine. The application is also designed with inclusivity in mind, catering to minority users such as individuals with learning disabilities such as dyslexia, ADHD, and visual impairments. Through features like screen readers, voice commands, and customizable fonts, the application provides essential support and accommodations for students requiring additional assistance, ensuring a more accessible and inclusive learning environment.

This app will effectively address the need for an accessible productivity application, using a simplistic yet intuitive design coupled with an ad-free experience. Moreover, all where the features of the app will not be barred by monthly fees or subscriptions like its competitors, thus fostering inclusivity. Our web application, although rooted in a similar concept to other websites, will provide a fresh, innovative, and accessible alternative to other flashcard websites, whilst allowing for expansive user customization entailing different study modes, cross-platform compatibility, progress tracking, deck organization, color themes, and more.

Notable Features

  • Creating and deleting flashcards, decks, and folders: Users will be able to create their own custom flashcards for both the front and back sides. Additionally, they will be able to group individual cards into decks, and decks into folders to allow better organization for studying and coursework.
  • Search support: From the user's created flashcards, decks, and folders, they will be able to search for keywords to efficiently find the materials and topics they need.
  • Studying and reviewing cards: In study mode, the app will show the users the cards they created to assist in studying. When a user begins studying a deck of cards, a timer will automatically begin timing them. When a user hovers over the displayed card, it will flip to the back and display the information. Additionally, the user can mark whether they rememebered the card correctly or if it was incorrect. Cards that were marked as incorrect can be revisited and reviewed later. Users can also shuffle their decks in order to provide more effective studying practicies.
  • Playing study music: Another section of the web app will be dedicated to providing distraction-free, calming music to assist with studying. Users can choose to play or pause the music.
  • Login: The app will support login and user authentication to provide user's with their own customized study space. All flashcards and other stored data will be linked to users' logins via Firebase.
