E-Flashcard WebApp

Problem/Data Domain

In academic settings, a prevalent challenge is students' difficulty in maintaining focus and developing effective learning methods. To address this, our proposal centers on creating an integrated study web application that incorporates e-flashcards, a study timer, study music, and customizable features, providing an alternative solution to enhance student’s learning and productivity.

Although productivity apps do exist, a large portion of the apps only have one functionality. Due to this, students have to install and use multiple apps and services to gain access to productivity tools. Opening and closing multiple apps can be a major hassle and it also provides an easy opportunity for students to get sidetracked from their studying.

Additionally, with the internet’s ease of accessibility, we often find ourselves watching internet videos or scrolling through social media when we are supposed to be studying, working on homework, or simply doing anything important. With our web app, we can tackle this issue by providing a space where users can study effectively and productively while preventing potential distractions.

App Description

The primary users of the application are college students seeking effective study tools to enhance their learning experience, improve productivity, and establish a structured study routine. The application is also designed with inclusivity in mind, catering to minority users such as individuals with learning disabilities such as dyslexia, ADHD, and visual impairments. Through features like screen readers, voice commands, and customizable fonts, the application provides essential support and accommodations for students requiring additional assistance, ensuring a more accessible and inclusive learning environment.

This app will effectively address the need for an accessible productivity application, using a simplistic yet intuitive design coupled with an ad-free experience. Moreover, all where the features of the app will not be barred by monthly fees or subscriptions like its competitors, thus fostering inclusivity. Our web application, although rooted in a similar concept to other websites, will provide a fresh, innovative, and accessible alternative to other flashcard websites, whilst allowing for expansive user customization entailing different study modes, cross-platform compatibility, progress tracking, deck organization, color themes, and more.
